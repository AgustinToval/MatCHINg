# MatCHINg

A mental math PWA with an infinite scroll of calculations to solve. Each card shows a problem (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, squares, percentages, and chained operations) — type the answer and press Enter to check it. Difficulty ramps up as you scroll, and your score, streak, and best streak are tracked at the top.

## Features

- Infinite scroll feed of randomly generated problems
- Increasing difficulty the further you go
- Installable as a Progressive Web App (manifest + service worker, works offline)
- Score, streak, and best-streak tracking

